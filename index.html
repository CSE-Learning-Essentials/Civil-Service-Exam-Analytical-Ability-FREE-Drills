PART 3: LOGICAL REASONING

PRACTICE TEST | 5-ITEM TEST | PASSING SCORE: 3

Directions: Read each question and choose the correct answer.

1. P and Q are sisters. R is P’s father. S is R’s wife. T is Q’s daughter. How is S related to T?

2. P is the brother of Q, R is the mother of P, and S is the father of R. How is P related to Q?

3. Pointing at a woman, a man said “She is the sister-in-law of the only daughter of my mother-in-law’s husband.” How is the woman related to the man?

4. P is the sister of Q; R is the brother of S; Q is the mother of R and T is the father of S. How is P related to S?

5. P is brother of Q. Q is sister of R. R is son of S. T is sister of S. How is T related to Q?

Explanations:

  1. A. Grandmother
    P and Q are sisters → same parents
    R is P’s father → also Q’s father
    S is R’s wife → S is their mother
    T is Q’s daughter → S is T’s grandmother.
  2. C. Brother
    P is brother of Q → directly states relationship.
    R is mother of P → R is also Q's mother.
    S is father of R → their grandfather. So, P is Q’s brother.
  3. C. Sister
    "Only daughter of my mother-in-law’s husband" = my wife.
    Woman is sister-in-law of my wife → she is my sister.
  4. B. Aunt
    P is Q’s sister → Q is the mother of R and S.
    P is maternal aunt of S.
  5. A. Aunty
    P, Q, and R are siblings. Their parent is S.
    T is S’s sister → T is their aunt → T is Q’s aunty.
